Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Gangnam Hakdang respects user privacy and handles information only as necessary to operate its content publishing application.

The application may access basic TikTok account information and publishing permissions when the account owner authorizes the application through TikTok.

Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with unauthorized third parties.

Data Retention

Authentication credentials and access tokens are retained only for as long as required to operate the publishing service and may be revoked by the account owner.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the application owner through the official Gangnam Hakdang account.
